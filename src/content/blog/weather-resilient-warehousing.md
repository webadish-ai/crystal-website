India's weather is getting more unpredictable every year. There are more heatwaves, floods, and storms than ever before. This makes it harder for companies to move and store goods safely. Because of this, having strong and safe warehouses is now more important than ever. These warehouses help keep products safe, make sure businesses don't lose money, and protect workers.

### Main Changes and What They Mean

#### 1. Warehouses Are More Than Just Storage

**Before:**

Warehouses were just places to keep goods until they were needed.

**Now:**

Warehouses have become important centres that protect goods from bad weather. They also help keep business running even when there are floods or storms.

#### 2. Stronger and Safer Buildings

**Before:**

Warehouses were built simply, with little thought about weather problems.

**Now:**

Warehouses need to be strong enough to survive storms and floods. They have better roofs, walls, and drainage to stop water from getting in. They also have systems to control temperature and keep goods safe from heat or humidity.

#### 3. Worker Safety and Emergency Plans

**Before:**

There weren't many rules about keeping workers safe during bad weather.

**Now:**

Warehouses must have clear plans for emergencies like floods or fires. Workers are trained on what to do, and there are alarms and safety gear available at all times.

#### 4. Using Technology to Stay Safe

**Before:**

Most warehouses used simple locks and basic security.

**Now:**

Warehouses use technology like sensors to watch for fires, floods, or temperature changes. Some use computers to warn staff if something goes wrong, so they can act quickly.
