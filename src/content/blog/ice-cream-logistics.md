### The Science and Soul Behind Storing Ice Cream

Ice cream is a delicate balance of air, fat, and flavour, all held together by the magic of freezing. To stay safe and delicious, it must be kept at **-18°C or below** from the moment it's churned until it reaches your hand. Even a brief rise in temperature can turn a creamy delight into a disappointing puddle — ruining not just the product, but the moment itself.

For decades, ice cream sellers — especially small businesses and those in remote areas — faced the heartbreak of melted stock and wasted effort. Without reliable cold storage and transport, the journey from factory to consumer was fraught with risk. Today, thanks to **advanced cold chain solutions**, that journey is smoother, safer, and sweeter than ever.
