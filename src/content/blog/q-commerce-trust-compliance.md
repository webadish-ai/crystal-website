The quick commerce industry is expanding rapidly, bringing convenience and speed to consumers' fingertips. To sustain this growth and maintain consumer trust, it is crucial for quick commerce players to prioritize food safety and compliance. Robust cold storage infrastructure, along with strict hygiene management and operational protocols, are key enablers to maintain high safety standards while meeting the need for speed.

### Key Regulatory Challenges Faced in Quick Commerce Operations

#### 1. Operating Without Mandatory Food Safety License

**Problem:**

Some quick commerce facilities operate without the required Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) license mandated under Section 31(1) of the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006 — despite handling packaged and perishable foods.

**Solution:**

Cold chain providers ensure full regulatory compliance, operating with all mandatory licenses, including FSSAI, to guarantee safe handling of perishable goods. This proactive adherence protects consumer interests and builds operational credibility from day one.

#### 2. Poor Food Safety & Hygiene Standards

**Problem:**

Issues such as food stored directly on the floor, dusty racks, absence of pest control audits, improperly dressed food handlers, and uncalibrated cold storage units compromised food safety and quality.

**Solution:**

Cold chain solutions maintain rigorous hygiene protocols, including elevated storage racks, calibrated refrigeration equipment, pest control certifications, AMC and staff trained in safe food handling. Continuous temperature monitoring and sealed environments help preserve product integrity and consumer safety consistently.

#### 3. Broader Quick Commerce Scrutiny

**Problem:**

Issues are flagged in quick commerce dark stores, showing a sector-wide need to improve hygiene and compliance.

**Solution:**

Cold chain companies offer nationwide support with documented evidence of hygiene and safety practices, enabling quick commerce players to easily satisfy intensified regulatory inspections. Integrated digital tracking and compliance tools facilitate seamless audit readiness and elevate industry standards.
